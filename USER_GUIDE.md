# User Guide

## Getting started

1. Go to the homepage and click **Sign up free**.
2. Enter your email and a password (8+ characters), accept the Terms of
   Use and Privacy Policy, and submit.
3. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link. You can't
   generate anything until your email is verified.
4. Sign in.

## LinkedIn Post Creator

1. From the homepage or Dashboard, open **LinkedIn Post Creator**.
2. Type your subject (up to 32 words) — be specific, e.g. "Why most SMEs
   run out of cash despite being profitable" works better than "cash flow."
3. Optionally check **Create a professional image for this post**.
4. Click **Generate post**.
5. Review the result. Use **Regenerate** for a fresh take, **Improve** to
   sharpen the current draft, or **Shorten** to tighten it up. You can also
   edit the text directly in the box.
6. When you're happy: **Copy** the post and paste it directly into
   LinkedIn (the bold formatting will paste correctly), or **Download
   Word/.txt** to save it first. Posting to LinkedIn itself is a manual
   step you do — Post2Hire doesn't publish on your behalf.
7. If you generated an image, **Download image** and attach it to your
   LinkedIn post manually.

## CV & Cover Letter Generator

1. Open **CV & Cover Letter Generator**.
2. Upload your CV (DOCX or PDF, under 5 MB).
3. Paste the full job description into the text box — there's no link
   option; copy the text directly from the job posting.
4. Choose what you want: **Tailor my CV**, **Cover letter**, or **Both**.
5. Enter your name and the role title (used to name your downloaded
   files).
6. Click **Generate**.
7. Review the result carefully, including any items under **Flagged for
   your review** — these are genuine gaps between the job's requirements
   and your CV that the tool won't paper over.
8. Check the confirmation box once you've reviewed everything, then
   download as **Word** or **PDF**.

## Managing your files

Your Dashboard's **Recent Files** shows everything you've generated. You
can rename, re-download, or delete individual items, or clear everything
at once.

## Account settings

From **Account Settings** you can change your password or permanently
delete your account (and everything in it — this can't be undone).
